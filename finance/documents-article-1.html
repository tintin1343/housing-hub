Documentation Needed for loan applications


Items typically required to apply for a home loan with SunTrust Mortgage include:

  • Pay stubs for the previous 30 days for all borrowers
  • Signed personal federal tax returns, including all schedules, for the past two years
  • W-2s/1099s for all employers for the past two years
  • Two months of current statements for all bank and/or credit union accounts showing balances and account numbers—to include all pages of the bank statement and explain/ document any large bank deposits
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Landlord history for the previous two years including name, address and phone number, as well as the monthly rent amount (if applicable)
  • Any additional financial records that may be relevant to the qualification, including bankruptcy, divorce decree, or judgments/ collection payments. Documentation of receipt of alimony/child support payments need to be provided only when you use it as income for loan qualification purposes.
