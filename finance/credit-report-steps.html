About Credit Reports


Credit Score is a very important factor when you apply for a mortgage or apply for any financing. Understanding what impacts your credit score and what your credit report contains can help you take the necessary steps you need to, get the perfect credit score.


Components of Credit Score

The following factors affect your credit score:

  1. Payment History
  2. Amounts Owed
  3. Length of Credit History
  4. New Credit
  5. Types of Credits used
Median range / Overall range
FICO Median Score is 723. The range of credit score is 350-850.

Full Credit Report

A full credit report is different. You can get a full report from credit reporting agencies: Equifax, Experian and TransUnion. This report contains:

This report contains the following:

  1. Credit History: Payment History, etc.
  2. Who else is checking up on your credit
  3. Any judgements against you. For example, bankruptcy.
  4. Personal Information
  5. A section for comments by you. This occurs if you have disputed the report in the past.
